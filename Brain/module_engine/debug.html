    Does Ystad Sweden have snow?
    Ystad experiences some seasonal variation in monthly snowfall. The snowy period of the year lasts for 3.0 months, from December 6 to March 6, with a sliding 31-day snowfall of at least 1.0 inches. The month with the most snow in Ystad is February, with an average snowfall of 1.5 inches.
